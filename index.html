The Project

Guardian Alert: Enhancing Safety with Real-Time Alerts

Guardian Alert is an IoT-based women and child safety system designed to provide immediate assistance in times of danger. By pressing a button on the device, the user can instantly send an alert notification to family members or friends, sharing their real-time location through the mobile app.

The system includes GPS tracking, a panic button feature, and integration with the Firebase Realtime Database to ensure fast and reliable communication. Our goal is to empower individuals with a tool that can offer peace of mind in everyday life or emergencies.

Live Location

Track the real-time location of the user with GPS, ensuring immediate access to their whereabouts in emergencies or unfamiliar areas.
